Mandarin Tone Learning


Mandarin Tone Learning is my final project for Machine Learning class. There are four tones in mandarin. It's is hard for some mandarin learners to disguish between tones and pronounce the right tone.

In this project, we wanted use machine learning to make a software that helps mandarin learners to know whether he or she is pronouncing the tone correctly. We used support vector machine as a classifier to train the data.
